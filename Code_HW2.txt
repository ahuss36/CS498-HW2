I used python to implement both parts of the script for part 4, with the requests lib to sychronize calls and time for milliseconds. The first part goes through each of our 4 combinations of US/EU instances and /register|/list endpoints. We take the start time, then find the end time after our call and subtract to get our latency. Then we repeat 10 times while summing each value and once we finish our loop, divide by 10 to get our final average. For the second part, we make a user on our US server, then immedieately check if it exists on our EU server. If it doesn't we increment our missed count, and repeat 100 times to return our final count.
